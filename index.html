About

To design human-centered interface with industry standards and make it intuitive is the what I look up to when it comes to Human-Computer Interaction. I cite my past developing/design experience and astute observational skills as my inspiration towards design.

Projects

Sheqscan is a tool with the help of which one could easily customise checklists, assign them to users for completion on the spot where they are required and at the same time eliminate unnecessary paperwork and endless hours of report writing. Sheqscan also trigger and pinpoint checklist completion with GPS co-ordinates and QR codes. You could report and close deviations on a continuous basis or even provide task related training tools on the spot to the user.

Business Risks International is trying to achieve with their app Widuress. The client needed ‘something’ for university students that in case of danger would send user location, user status, make a loud noise, etc. I tinkered with two ideas predominantly. One was a wearable gadget and one was an app. I decided against the wearable gadget as it would’ve been an additional cost for the client to manufacture/purchase and market as well as something a student would have to buy as opposed to a phone. I went with the app.
